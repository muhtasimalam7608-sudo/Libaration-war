Significance of the Archive

The 1971 Liberation War is the most significant event in the history of Bangladesh. This archive serves as a digital sanctuary for the memories of our martyrs and freedom fighters. Our mission is to collect, preserve, and showcase authentic historical records, ensuring that future generations can understand the price of our freedom and the bravery of the people.
